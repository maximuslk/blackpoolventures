About Us

Blackpool Ventures Private Limited was founded in 2022 to make a commercial impact in Trincomalee, a harbor town with one of the best beaches in the country with high potential for development. With many educated youths unemployed it was high time to put a plan for the economic revival of Trincomalee. Only with economic progress a nation can be better and hence doing what we can to improve the economy and the livelihood of people in Trincomalee. The founder of Blackpool is from this town, and he wants to make an impact on his soil.
